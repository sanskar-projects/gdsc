Greetings from Google Developers Student Clubs,
Thank you so much for your interest in Google Developer Student Club .
Google collaborates with university students who are passionate about growing developer communities and supports them with Google resources.
It is open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advance developers who want to further enhance their skills.
It is intended to be a platform for students to learn and collaborate as they solve problems around them with help of technology.
Lets-Learn-Connect-Grow together!
